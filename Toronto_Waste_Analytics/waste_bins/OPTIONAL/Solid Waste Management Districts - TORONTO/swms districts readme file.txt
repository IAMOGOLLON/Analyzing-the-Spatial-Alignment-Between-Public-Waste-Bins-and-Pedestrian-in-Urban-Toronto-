Solid Waste Districts: There are a total of 4 districts in the City of Toronto	

FID = Internal Record Identifier software generated field	
SHAPE = Polygon -software generated field
AREAS_ID = unique geographic identifier GEO_ID
AREA_NAME = District Name (Formerly known as NAME)
AREA_SHORT = Solid Waste Distric Number (Formerly known as SCODE_NAME)
AREA_LONG_	= Solid Waste Distric Number (Formerly known as LCODE_NAME)
AREA_DESC = solid waste management districts (Formerly known as TYPE_DESC)
